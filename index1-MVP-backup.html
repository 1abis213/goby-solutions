FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about buying appliances in Montreal.

Do you deliver appliances in Montreal?
Yes, we offer free delivery in Montreal and paid delivery to surrounding areas including Laval, Longueuil, the South Shore, and the West Island. Our team schedules delivery around your availability and handles careful placement in your home.
What brands do you carry?
We carry Samsung, LG, Bosch, Whirlpool, GE, Maytag, KitchenAid, Frigidaire, and Electrolux — both new and certified pre-owned models.
Do you sell certified pre-owned appliances?
Yes. Every pre-owned appliance we sell is thoroughly inspected, cleaned, and tested before it reaches our showroom floor. We stand behind them with the same after-sales support as our new units.
Do you offer appliance installation?
Yes, we can arrange professional installation for your new or pre-owned appliance. Ask our team about installation options when you call.
Where is your showroom located?
We are located at 15 Rue Legendre O, Montreal, QC H2N 1G9, in the Villeray\u2013Saint-Michel\u2013Parc-Extension area, near the intersection of Rue Legendre and Boulevard de l\u2019Acadie. Visit us Monday to Saturday.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept cash, credit card, debit card, Interac e-Transfer, and wire transfer. Ask us about payment plan options when you visit the showroom.
